Importance of Freshwater

Freshwater is essential to life, yet it's under constant threat. Explore the impact of pollution on our lakes and water quality through interactive simulations. Play, learn, and see firsthand how our actions affect the health of aquatic ecosystems and the future of clean water.

How to Use the Simulation

  • • Pollute: Click "Pollute" to add pollutants and see their effects.
  • • Clean: Click "Clean" to remove pollutants and restore the environment.
  • • Learn: New facts appear with each action—click the links to explore more.
  • • Data: View how pollution and cleaning affect the environment in the "Data" section.
  • • Reset: Click "Reset" to return to the original state.
